➤ Switch Axe :※ Switch Axe F (F is for Frontier)(SA, SwA, Swaxe) is a very strong offensive and defensive weapon. With a Parry and Fade Slashes than can even be used after another, it is incredibly powerful when mastered, and you can become fully untouchable while destroying the monster.
※ You need to actively parry to sustain the Switch gage, otherwise you will lose a lot of damage potential.
