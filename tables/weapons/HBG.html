※ Heavy Bowgun (HBG) has many mecanics for its own. It can be played regularly by spamming bullets (pierces recomended), or load an entire magazine for a single powerful shot.
※ Positioning and safety are extremly important for HBG as to both survive your lack of defense, and finding the right angle uppon the monster to deal the most damage output possible.
※ Pick a playstyle before building a set: Regular playstyle for ammos spamming, or Heat Beam focused playstyle using skills buffing specifically the beam and make the build-up faster.

Spacing×Spacing10Alters critical distance for Normal,Pierce,Pellet and Blunt shots to be lower and further increases damage at critical distance.
If you consistently attack within this critical distance you gain Movement Speed+2 and Evade Distance Up.
LBG and HBG get increased perfect shot (3 Segments) and compression windows (2 segments). Bow gets lowered charge times that stack with Auto-Reload.
Does not buff attacks without Critical Distance, is added to the buff for the first half of HBG's Critical Distance.
Buff builds an internal meter that fills in much the same way as Thunder Clad and total hits needed will vary with Attack, Affinity, Shot Type, etc. but meter build up is not reset on being launched etc.		 The critical distance alteration is a must have as it will give you far easier positioning and allows you to shot much further and closer to the monster while keeping your damage up, and assure all the ticks from pierce ammo fit in critical distance.
Increased Compression Windows segment is a great QoL and help making sure you do not miss your timing when reloading the gun. Since the Compression Shot bar is bigger on both sides, the left side of the segment will be larger and so can allow slighty sooner reload than usual.
※ However Evade Distance Up is naturally given by the hiden HBG skill, and Thunder Clad's movement speed buff is still the go to when it comes to movement.
※ This means the buff side of this skill is not really important on HBG, what really matters is the critical distance alternation.

Thunder Clad×Thunder Clad10You gain a gauge below the character name and it is accumulated by evading, attacking, and moving.
When the gauge reaches its maximum you gain the following skills and benefits for 60 seconds.
All raw hitzones +5 (this stacks with other sources), Status Negate, Movement Speed Up+2, Weapon Handling, and Evade Distance Up.
If you take a hit that launches you before the bar was filled you will lose all progress.		 Half the skills provided by Thunder Clad are gained already by other must have skills : Movement Speed+2 is provided by Spacing, Evade Distance Up is provided by the hiden skill, and Weapon Handling is provided by Drawing Arts.

Rush×Rush10Successful attacks and guard actions gradually increase an invisible meter that has two stages.
Stage one: Chest flashes and gains purple effect, giving +50 attack. Stage two: Flash repeats, gains +130 attack total, and running with weapons no longer consumes stamina.
The effect is completely reset when you use any items or sheathe your weapon.
The attack meter builds based on the Motion Value of the attacks used. As damage is not involved, this means you can build up Rush on a dead monster.
Ranged400 values20000 values
Ranged weapons take into account all multipliers (Critical, Critical Distance, Bullet, Charge) and Raw Attack for stages.
※ e.g. Firing a Normal lv.1 shot with a 1140 Attack LBG using a set with an Origin piece, Steady Hand and Issen+3, you would get Stage 1 in a single shot, and stage 2 in around 46 shots (0.12 x 1.5 x 1.8 x 1.1 x 1140 = 400).
※ With stage 2 active, the Extreme Style run has no stamina consumption but will not work if your stamina is literally 0.		 Rush is free damage on HBG as in theory it should stay active for the whole quest.
Since you should always positionate safely as gunner, you do not require to use items and you will not sheathe at all, making Rush furthermore reliable.
