※ Magnet Spike (MS) is insanely powerful and there is nothing that it cannot do. Fast evades with instant iframes, a block that lasts almost a full second, and a way to literally lock down monsters.
※ Use the pin move as much as you can when playing with a team as it gives the whole team free damages for roughly 5 seconds, and should absolutly not be slept on.
※ The blunt mode is not to forget, you can easily stun the monster after a pin. Dishing out ton of damage while giving opportunities for your team is MS true strength.
