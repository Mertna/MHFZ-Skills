➤ Active Feature :※ Each day, two weapons are selected to receive passive buffs until next day (Taiwanese timezone). MS is the only weapon that does not have an active feature.
※ You can figure out which weapons got their active feature up when looking at the top of your screen while being in the plazza.
Sword and Shield (SnS)Status and Elemental attack increased 1.2x. This is internal only and does not change the value on your weapon status.
Dual Swords (DS)Stamina and HP consumption lowered (0.5x).
Greatsword (GS)Unsheathe attacks gain +100% affinity. This is additional and goes on top of any existing affnity and works with Critical Conversion.
This also applies to Extreme Style attacks from Parries, and gives the raw increasing effect of skill Critical Conversion (no +30%) while you are performing these actions.
Longsword (LS)Full spirit bar buff effect increased (+10 atk > +40 atk).
Hammer (Hammer)Stun damage increased 1.5x, stacks with Sigil or Caravan Skills.
Hunting Horn (HH)Song duration 2.0x, stacks with Flute expert.
Lance (Lance)Guard Skill goes up by 1 level. Activates Reflect +3.
Gunlance (GL)Wyvern Fire and Shelling Damage increased 1.5x.
Switch Axe (SA)Increases phial meter recovery by primary recovery mechanic (Reloading or Guarding).
Tonfa (Tonfas)All Ryuuki finisher (explosion) effects are buffed.
(Head) KO damage duration: 20s > 30s. | (Tail) Bleeding: 1.5x Damage. | (Body) Sharpness return: 15 > 20. | Gunner Attack Up: 25 > 50.
Light Bowgun (LBG)Damage increased at critical distance (+0.1x) and Status damage 1.2x.
Heavy Bowgun (HBG)Damage increased at critical distance (+0.1x).
Bow (Bow)Short Charge effect which stacks with Auto-Reload (0.85x).
