➤ Caravan Gem Skills :※ Once you got your Caravan Gem (visit the Caravan atleast once), you can equip up to 3 passive skills, worth a total of 10 cost points. Activated skills turn orange.
※ You should always look to upgrade your Caravan Gem during your playthrough, as Caravan Skills are entirely passive skills and thus free bonus. Pick the rainbow color every day.
※ Skills using the following color          are the only skills you should look for, others are either gimmick, either not worth using. Beginners should always pick 'Recovery Items Up'.
