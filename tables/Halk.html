➤ Support Halk :※ The Halk is your flying pet. It can have up to 2 skills equipped until GR150, and can equip a 3rd one past GR150. Equip them by talking to the NPC in "My Support" area.
※ At the end of a quest, the Halk occasionally drops a shinie. Make sure to harvest it as it has a chance to unlock a new skill for the Halk.
-Halk Behavior-
Small Monster PriorityAll questsThe Halk will give priority to attacking small monsters regardless of the distance from its partner hunter.
Large Monster PriorityAll questsThe Halk will give priority to attacking large monsters regardless of the distance from its partner hunter.
Near Hunter PriorityAll questsThe Halk will give priority to attacking near its partner hunter.
Always AggressiveAll questsThe Halk will always attack regardless of whether the partner hunter is sheathed or not.
UnescapableAll questsWhen the Halk attacks a target, it will keep attacking this target without changing for a while.
Item DefenderAll questsWhen the partner hunter uses an item, the Halk will attack monsters nearby them.
Carving DefenderAll questsAttacks monsters around its partner hunter while they are carving.
Health ConsciousAll questsWhen the Halk's HP decreases, it stops attacking to perform its health recovery action.
Signal to Halt AttackingAll questsThe Halk will stop attacking if its partner hunter uses the gesture「Signal」.
Gathering AffectionHC questsThe Halk will no longer attack but will drop items instead.
-Halk Support Abilities-
Helper [Health]All questsWhen the partner hunter's HP is low, the Halk performs a health recovery action on them. The Halk will also heal other hunters in the quest.
Helper [Antidote]All questsIf the partner hunter becomes poisoned, the Halk performs a poison recovery action on them. The Halk will also cure other hunters in the quest.
Helper [Deoderant]All questsIf the partner hunter becomes stinky, the Halk performs a deodorant action on them. The Halk will also clean other hunters in the quest.
Monster SpotterHR30+ questsThe Halk sometimes gives the effect of a psychoserum to its partner hunter.
MapperHR30+ questsThe Halk's partner hunter can see the entire map wether they possess a map in their pouch or not.
Demon Drug DustHC questsThe Halks gives its partner hunter the same effect as Demon Drug. The Halk will also buff other hunters in the quest.
Armor Drug DustHC questsThe Halks gives its partner hunter the same effect as Armor Drug. The Halk will also buff other hunters in the quest.
SupporterHC questsThe Halk attempts to break hunter out of any cancellable status (stun, snowman, crystallisation..).
Contain MonsterGR questsLarge monsters are less likely to move areas.
