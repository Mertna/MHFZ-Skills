➤ Sigil Skills :※ Sigil Skills are activated by equipping a Sigil on a weapon. Sigils take a deco slot, and are noticeable by a diamond shape instead of the usual decoration circle shape.
※ Rare sigils are incredibly powerful and can heavily change the way you are going to use a weapon, by giving you more stats, more range, change the weapon mecanics, and more.
※ However, getting a good sigil relies on luck. When rolling a sigil, it is going to get randomized values for its skills, ranging usually from -10 to +15, depending the sigil.
※ Sigil skills without value are active immediatly if the corresponding sigil is equipped. For other sigil skills, higher the value, better and rarer the sigil is.
